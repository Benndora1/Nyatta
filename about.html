Our Story

A Decade of Excellence in Event Planning

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate event professionals, Nyatta Events began with a simple mission: to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories. What started as a small boutique planning service has grown into one of the region's most trusted event management companies.

Our journey has been marked by countless successful events, from intimate gatherings to large-scale corporate conferences. Each event has taught us something new, refined our processes, and strengthened our commitment to excellence.

Today, we're proud to have planned over 500 events, maintained a 98% client satisfaction rate, and built lasting relationships with clients who trust us with their most important moments.

Our Mission

To create exceptional events that exceed expectations through innovative planning, meticulous execution, and personalized service that reflects each client's unique vision.

Our Vision

To be the premier event planning company recognized for transforming celebrations into unforgettable experiences that bring people together and create lasting memories.

Our Values

Excellence, integrity, creativity, and client-focused service guide everything we do. We believe every event tells a story, and we're honored to help tell yours.
