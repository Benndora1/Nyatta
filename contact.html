Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings and major events, though we can accommodate shorter timelines based on availability.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to make our services accessible. We'll work with you to create a payment schedule that fits your budget.

What's included in your consultation?

Our initial consultation includes event vision discussion, budget planning, timeline creation, and a customized proposal at no charge.

Do you work with specific vendors?

We have preferred vendor partners but are happy to work with your chosen vendors or help you find new ones that fit your style and budget.
